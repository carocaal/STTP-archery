Our Lessons

Explore the different ways to experience archery with us. From first-time beginners to seasoned archers, kids' programs, and team‑building sessions for companies, we have a class designed for every level and audience.

Lesson list

Beginners

No prerequisites—just enthusiasm. This class covers stance, bow handling, aiming, and safe shooting basics. You'll get a clear overview of range rules, guided practice, and all equipment provided, including bows, arrows, and protective gear. Coach Jhon will be leading the session, ensuring every step is safe, supportive, and rewarding.

Scheduled Saturdays and Sundays at 11:00am and 12:00pm

Kids lessons

Lessons for children ages 8-16. Focus on safety, fundamentals, and fun. Coach Ana will guide kids through engaging activities that build confidence and skills. Parents are welcome to observe and support their young archers in a friendly, encouraging environment.
